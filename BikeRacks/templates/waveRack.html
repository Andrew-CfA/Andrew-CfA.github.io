Wave Rack

About:

The Wave Rack comes in last in terms of number found throughout the boroughs - coming in at 56, you'll be hard pressed to catch this wave (heh) of bike racks.

The design of the wave rack is very similar to the U rack, a wave rack can be described as U - racks attached together, making a wave.
