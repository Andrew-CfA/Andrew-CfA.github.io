Quick Facts

Client: City of Fort Collins

URL: https://getfoco.fcgov.com/

GitHub: https://github.com/Get-Your/GetFoco

Category: Web App (SAAS)

Tech: Django Backend; JavaScript Frontend; GitHub; Azure Hosting; Docker Containers; Twilio SMS; Google Analytics;

Get FoCo is Fort Collins' first income-qualified programs web-app SAAS designed to help its residents in need!

This project was born out of the frustrations and anxieties that I lived through during 2020 - 2021, with the world reeling in from Covid, it was obvious that government services needed updating. Get FoCo was designed to treat its clients not as a statistic, but as people.
